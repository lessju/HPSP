Food and Beverage
Breakfast is served at our Spice Island restaurant on level 4 from 6:30am till 10:30am, whilst lunch is typically served at our Admiral’s Landing restaurant on level 5.

A standard coffee break consists of freshly brewed coffee, a selection of teas, orange juice and freshly baked pastries/cookies at Eur7.00 per person, to be served in the meeting room foyer. (Venue images attached)

Should you wish to organise any group lunches, prices commence at Eur14.50 per person for a light standing lunch, which may be set up in the meeting room foyer area or possibly at the Chartroom Bar, which is a very popular space as it overlooks Marsamxett harbour and has direct access to a lovely terrace (Image attached).

Plated menus commence at Eur26.00 per person, whilst buffet menus commence at Eur30.00 per person (excluding beverages).

Dinner is typically served at our Admiral’s Landing restaurant, where guests may opt for our half board supplement of Eur30.00 per person which would entitle them to a lavish buffet menu.

A beverage package of say ½ wine, ½ water and coffee may be added for Eur12.00 per person.

We also have some lovely outdoor venues available such as our Rotunda for instance for 50 pax, which overlooks our pool area as well as Marsamxett Harbour. A Eur5.00 per person set up fee is applicable when selecting an outdoor venue (Venue image attached). A BBQ menu may also be worth looking into, with prices commencing at Eur28.50 per person excluding beverages.
